Travel Log app

Welcome to TravelLog, a dynamic travel journal app designed to document and share your adventures. Built with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js), TravelLog allows users to create, manage, and explore travel entries with ease. Whether you're capturing memories from a weekend getaway or a world tour, TravelLog provides an intuitive platform to chronicle your journeys, upload photos, and interact with a community of fellow travelers. With seamless performance and a user-friendly interface, TravelLog is your ultimate companion for preserving and sharing travel experiences.
